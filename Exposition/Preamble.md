# Jingqing’s Peck and Tap

A chick ready to hatch pecks from within the shell; the hen answers from without. Zen calls their meeting *peck and tap at once*.

A monk said to Jingqing, “The student pecks from within. I ask the teacher to tap from without.”

Jingqing said, “Will you come alive?”

The monk said, “If I do not, people will laugh at me.”

Jingqing said, “You too are a man in the weeds.”

— *Blue Cliff Record*, Case 16, “Jingqing’s Peck and Tap”

*(rendered from the Chinese)*
